Rules:

* Players take turns dropping colored discs into a seven-column, six-row grid.
 
* To drop a discs, a player touches a column of the grid.

* The discs fall straight down, occupying the next available space within the column.

* The objective of the game is to connect four of one's own discs of the same color next to each other vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before the opponent. 

Good Luck.
